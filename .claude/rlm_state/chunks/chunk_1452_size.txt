*   **Keep Decorators Simple:** Decorators should be focused and perform a single, well-defined task.

# Common Pitfalls and How to Avoid Them

*   **Incorrect Argument Handling:** Ensure that your decorator correctly handles all possible arguments of the decorated function. Use `*args` and `**kwargs` to handle any positional and keyword arguments.
